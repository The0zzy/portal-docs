---
title: "ClearAllCustomMessages"
draft: false
---
Clears all custom messages for a provided **Player** or **TeamId**. If no **Player** or **TeamId** is provided, it will clear all custom messages for everyone.
### Inputs
1. **Player** | **TeamId**
    **(Group)**
    **_Optional_**
