---
title: "EnableGameModeObjective"
draft: false
---
Enables or disables the provided **CapturePoint** or **MCOM**.  
  
_Note: MCOMs are only available on TDM, VTDM, and Custom Conquest layouts._
### Inputs
1. **CapturePoint** | **MCOM** 
2. **Bool**
