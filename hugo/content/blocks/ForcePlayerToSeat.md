draft: false
---
Forces the specified **Player** into the target **Vehicle** at the provided seat **Number**.  
  
_Note: If the provided index is -1, that **Player** will be forced into the first available seat._
### Inputs
1. **Player**
2. **Vehicle**
3. **Number**
